# Process Map

## Core Processes
- **Opportunity and Proposal Management**
  - Inputs: customer enquiry, statement of work requirements, risk assessment triggers.
  - Outputs: proposals, estimates, contracts, agreed acceptance criteria.
  - Interfaces: feeds Service Delivery; receives risk insights from Risk Management and lessons learned from Continual Improvement.
- **Service Delivery and Project Execution**
  - Inputs: approved contracts, scope and requirements, project plan, documented controls.
  - Outputs: deliverables, status reports, change requests, acceptance records.
  - Interfaces: consumes Document Control assets; provides performance data to Management Review and Customer Feedback.
- **Customer Feedback and Issue Management**
  - Inputs: satisfaction surveys, complaints, ad-hoc feedback, monitoring alerts.
  - Outputs: corrective actions, improvement opportunities, updates to knowledge base.
  - Interfaces: supplies data to Continual Improvement and Management Review; informs Risk Management where issues introduce new risks.

## Support Processes
- **Risk Management**
  - Inputs: project risks, supplier risks, security findings, change requests.
  - Outputs: risk register entries, treatment plans, residual risk approvals.
  - Interfaces: informs Opportunity Management, Service Delivery, and Management Review; receives incident information from Customer Feedback.
- **Document Control**
  - Inputs: policy updates, templates, records from delivery activities.
  - Outputs: version-controlled documents, published templates, archived records.
  - Interfaces: supports all core processes; receives improvement actions from Continual Improvement.
- **Supplier and Resource Management**
  - Inputs: competency requirements, supplier evaluations, contract terms.
  - Outputs: approved supplier list, onboarding records, performance reviews.
  - Interfaces: provides qualified resources to Service Delivery and receives feedback from Customer Feedback and Management Review.
- **Management Review and Continual Improvement**
  - Inputs: KPI dashboards, audit findings, feedback, nonconformities, risk status, resource updates.
  - Outputs: decisions, action plans, updated objectives, policy changes.
  - Interfaces: drives updates to all processes; monitors the effectiveness of actions and closes items once verified.

## Interaction Overview
- Opportunities flow into Service Delivery once accepted; Document Control provides the standard artefacts to start work.
- Service Delivery produces outputs that feed Customer Feedback and KPI tracking; nonconformities trigger Risk Management and improvement actions.
- Management Review consolidates metrics and feedback, directing changes to objectives, processes, and documented policies.
- Supplier and Resource Management supports both Opportunity Management and Service Delivery, ensuring capability and availability.
